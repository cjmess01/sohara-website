SoHara's Mission

To lead the industry in providing battery State of Health estimation products to enable safer, longer lasting, and more affordable energy storage.

SoHara's Vision

A world where battery energy storage is used to its fullest potential, enabling advancements in artificial intelligence, electric vehicles, and grid reliability.

SoHara's Story

SoHara was founded in the Summer of 2025 by Shane Vanderhagen and Caleb Messerly. Mr. Shane Vanderhagen is a Co-founder and Chief Executive Officer, who received a BS degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) and is pursuing an MBA at The University of Alabama (UA); Mr. Caleb Messerly is a Co-founder and Chief Operating Officer, who received an MS degree in CS and is pursuing an MBA at UA. Both Shane and Caleb gained hands-on experience in power electronics, software development, and embedded systems through laboratory research conducted at UA. During this work, they were introduced to findings with the potential to address the growing challenges stemming from poor battery State-of-Health (SoH) estimation.

In the next 5 years, SoHara’s vision is to become a cornerstone of the energy storage system industry and enable battery energy storage to be the standard technology used to power America’s infrastructure. Through the completion of the outlined technical objectives, SoHara will position itself to provide the cutting-edge capability of rapid and embedded SoH estimation to make this a reality. In achieving this vision, SoHara will impact not only the viability of American energy storage systems but also the national competitiveness in other downstream concerns, including artificial intelligence, grid reliability, and energy independence.
